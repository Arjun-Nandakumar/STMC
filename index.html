Quality Management System
(ISO 9001:2015)



Quality Management System (QMS), also known as ISO 9001:2015, is an international standard that provides a framework for effective processes aimed at achieving customer satisfaction.

Why is it required?


ISO 9001 is required because it will help organisations address all aspects of managing the quality of their products and services with emphasis on customer satisfaction.


Ideal For :


  • All categories of employees in any industry
  • Educational institutions
  • Newly formed companies

Benefits offered :


  • Reduces rework
  • Reduces rejections
  • Increased customer trust and satisfaction
  • Increase in repetitive orders

What will you learn?


  • Improvement in communication
  • Doing things the right way
  • Reduced work

Environment Management System
(ISO 14001:2015)



Environment Management System referred briefly as EMS is covered under ISO 14001-2015 , an international standard, is a set of processes and practices that enables an organisation to to reduce its EMS impacts and increases its operating efficiency.

Why is it required?


EMS is required to reduce the impacts on the natural environment and comply withenvironmental requirements.


Ideal For :


Employees (managers, engineers and workers) who work in industries where the activities have an impact on the environment.

Benefits offered :


  • Reduced environmental impact
  • Improved operational performance
  • Reduction in waste
  • Compliance with regulatory requirements

What will you learn?


  • Mindset to reduce environmental impacts
  • Knowledge of products that have direct impact on environment

Good Manufacturing Practices
(ISO 22716:2007)



Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) is addressed in ISO 22716:2007. GMP certication is applicable for cosmetic industries, medical and pharmaceutical industries.

Why is it required?


Focus more on ensuring the safety and efficacy of products in regulated industries such as pharmaceutical, medical devices, food and cosmetic industries.


Ideal For :


  • Employees of cosmetics/food/pharmaceutical industries
  • Employees engaged in the manufacturing, processing, testing, packaging, labelling and storage or distribution of products

Benefits offered :


  • Maintain consistency and prevent contamination in food industries
  • Helps to prevent instances of contamination, mix-ups, deviations, failures and errors, for pharmaceutical company
  • Increased sales and brand loyalty

What will you learn?


  • Learn to produce goods correctly
  • Reduce mix-up or contamination
  • Reduce errors

Food Safety Management System (FSMS)
(ISO 22000:2018)



FSMS stands for Food Safety Management System, and is covered under ISO 22000-2018. It is applicable to all food chain industries.

Why is it required?


FSMS is required to ensure that every process, procedure, and resource is monitored for potential hazards.


Ideal For :


  • Food Safety managers and employees
  • Top management and functional heads
  • Employees whose role directly impacts food safety performance
  • Individuals who conduct internal and external auditing on FSMS

Benefits offered :


  • Reduces risk of contamination
  • Adherence to food safety regulations and standards
  • Increased customer trust and brand reputation
  • Minimize recalls, reworks, wastes, etc.

What will you learn?


  • How to induct safety in food management
  • How to build brand management
  • Doing things the right way
