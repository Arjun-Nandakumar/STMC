Non-Destructive Testing



Non-Destructive Testing, briefly referred as NDT, is a technique by which the soundness of any material can be assessed without destructing it.

Why is it required?


  • NDT techniques are desired in those cases where it is possible to assess how sound the materials are, and is void of any defects or not.
    Oscilloscopes are used to assess this.

Ideal For :


  • QA/QC technicians/engineers
  • Employees who wants to learn the techniques of NDT, which are MPI, UT, DPT, etc.

Benefits offered :


  • Can use defect-free material in assembly
  • Can know the defects in the material before it is processed
  • Reduce rejections at the last minute

What will you learn?


  • How to interpret the defects
  • Classify the defects as major or minor
  • Teach others on the usage of techniques

📅 Course Duration : 4 days


Mode : Both In-person & Remote
